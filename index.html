Upper Valley Chess

Bringing competitive chess to the Upper Valley

Mission

Our mission is to be a home for competitive chess in the upper valley. While that includes Hanover, Lebanon/West Lebanon, and White River Junction, we aim to strengthen the network of chess tournaments across Vermont and New Hampshire, as well as the broader New England Northeast. Everyone is welcome at our tournaments and events, regardless of where you are from!

Philosophy

It is our firm belief that the best way of getting better at chess is to play more tournament games, more consistently. Given this, we aim to hold a tournament each month so that there's year-round oppurtunity to play competitive games and grow in both skill and experience.
